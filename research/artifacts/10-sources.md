## Source-quality notes for the updating LLM
- **Prefer 🟢 primary docs** when stating doctrine/definitions; use 🟡 practitioner sources for technique; treat 🟠 (Wikipedia/Grokipedia/market-research/single blogs) as **leads to verify**, not authorities.
- **Dated/forward-looking items** (C2PA 2.1/ISO, OpenAI provenance timeline, "2026" tool posts) reflect a fast-moving space — re-verify before encoding as fact.
- The **"70–90% of intelligence is OSINT"** figure and **market-size** numbers are widely cited but weakly sourced — attribute, don't assert.
