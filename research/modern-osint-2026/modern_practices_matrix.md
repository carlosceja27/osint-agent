# Modern OSINT Practices Matrix

Date: 2026-06-08

| Practice area | Modern methods | Source/tool categories | Verification controls | Risks/failure modes | Representative sources | Skill implication |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Requirements and scoping | Define question, subject, jurisdiction, time window, exclusions, stop rules. | Intake forms, collection plans. | User approval gate; scope summary. | Mission creep, privacy harm. | [GOV-01: Intelligence Community OSINT Strategy 2024–2026](https://www.dni.gov/index.php/newsroom/reports-publications/reports-publications-2024/3897-the-ic-osint-strategy-2024-2026); [GOV-05: Open-source internet research](https://www.college.police.uk/app/investigation/investigative-strategies/internet-intelligence-and-investigations/open-source-internet-research) | Add collection-requirements artifact before searching. |
| Baseline web search | Query operators, multilingual variants, date filters, filetype/domain filters. | Search engines, archives. | Query log; source tier; archive important hits. | SEO spam, duplicates, stale pages. | [PRO-03: Online Research Tools](https://gijn.org/resource/online-research-tools/); [PRO-15: Internet Archive Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/) | Preserve exact queries and stop conditions. |
| Public records | Search official registries before aggregators. | EDGAR, Companies House, sanctions, ABN, NZ, Canada registries. | Verify with primary record; record jurisdiction/date. | Misreading legal status, stale records. | [GOV-11: Companies House Find and update company information](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/); [GOV-12: SEC EDGAR Full Text Search](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/search/); [GOV-16: Canada Business Registries search](https://beta.canadasbusinessregistries.ca/search); [GOV-17: Australian Business Register ABN Lookup](https://abr.business.gov.au/); [GOV-18: New Zealand Companies Register](https://companies-register.companiesoffice.govt.nz/) | Add source-tier and jurisdiction caveats. |
| Social/platform OSINT | Account discovery, username continuity, profile history, post provenance. | Platform search, archives, public APIs if allowed. | Original post, account history, cross-source corroboration. | Impersonation, deleted content, context collapse. | [ACAD-03: Information Disorder: Toward an interdisciplinary framework for research and policymaking](https://rm.coe.int/information-disorder-report-november-2017/1680764666); [PRO-04: The Verification Handbook](https://verificationhandbook.com/) | Add provenance and account-authenticity checks. |
| Image/video verification | Reverse image, frame extraction, EXIF, geolocation, shadow/weather/map corroboration. | Verification tools, maps, archives. | Original source, timestamp, location, manipulation indicators. | Reuploads, AI media, wrong location. | [GOV-03: Berkeley Protocol on Digital Open Source Investigations](https://www.ohchr.org/en/publications/policy-and-methodological-publications/berkeley-protocol-digital-open-source-investigations); [PRO-04: The Verification Handbook](https://verificationhandbook.com/); [PRO-05: First Draft Essential Guide to Verifying Online Information](https://firstdraftnews.org/long-form-article/essential-guide-to-verifying-online-information/) | Require verification notes for visual evidence. |
| Misinformation analysis | Classify claim/content/source, distinguish misinformation/disinformation/malinformation. | Fact-checking methods, platform context, source-quality research. | Original claim capture, chronology, independent corroboration. | Overlabeling, partisan framing, virality bias. | [ACAD-03: Information Disorder: Toward an interdisciplinary framework for research and policymaking](https://rm.coe.int/information-disorder-report-november-2017/1680764666); [ACAD-04: Defining “Fake News”: A typology of scholarly definitions](https://doi.org/10.1080/21670811.2017.1360143); [GOV-19: EUvsDisinfo methodology](https://euvsdisinfo.eu/methodology/) | Add precise terminology and evidence threshold. |
| Cyber/public infrastructure OSINT | WHOIS/RDAP, DNS, CT logs, Shodan, public advisories, threat reports. | crt.sh, Shodan, ENISA, vendor reports. | No exploitation; public-only; defensive relevance. | Unauthorized scanning/exploitation drift. | [PRO-10: Shodan Help Center](https://help.shodan.io/); [PRO-14: crt.sh Certificate Search](https://crt.sh/); [GOV-08: ENISA Threat Landscape 2024](https://www.enisa.europa.eu/publications/enisa-threat-landscape-2024) | Add “defensive public exposure discovery only” boundary. |
| Link/entity analysis | Map relationships from public records; graph entities with cited edges. | Maltego, Aleph, OpenCorporates, ICIJ. | Every edge must cite source and confidence. | Spurious connections, guilt by association. | [PRO-09: Maltego blog and resources](https://www.maltego.com/blog/); [PRO-06: OCCRP Aleph](https://aleph.occrp.org/); [PRO-07: ICIJ Offshore Leaks Database](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/); [PRO-13: OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/) | Require sourced edge table before graph conclusions. |
| Breach exposure self-audit | Check known emails/phones in notification services. | HIBP, identity monitoring, official breach notices. | Do not access raw dumps; record service/date/data types. | Handling stolen data, privacy exposure. | [PRO-16: Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) | Keep curated-service-only rule. |
| Automation | Batch discovery, entity extraction, monitoring, deduplication. | SpiderFoot, Maltego, APIs, scripts. | Human review, false-positive tracking, scope filters. | Noise, overcollection, API restrictions. | [PRO-11: SpiderFoot documentation](https://github.com/smicallef/spiderfoot); [PRO-09: Maltego blog and resources](https://www.maltego.com/blog/); [ACAD-01: The Not Yet Exploited Goldmine of OSINT: Opportunities, Open Challenges and Future Trends](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9139412) | Require validation status for automated hits. |
| AI-assisted OSINT | Query planning, summarization, extraction, translation, report drafting. | LLMs, OCR, entity extraction. | LLM is not a source; citations and primary-source checks required. | Hallucination, leakage, false citations. | [GOV-01: Intelligence Community OSINT Strategy 2024–2026](https://www.dni.gov/index.php/newsroom/reports-publications/reports-publications-2024/3897-the-ic-osint-strategy-2024-2026); [ACAD-01: The Not Yet Exploited Goldmine of OSINT: Opportunities, Open Challenges and Future Trends](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9139412) | Add AI controls and “facts must cite source” rule. |
| Reporting | Findings table, confidence, relevance, limitations, what was not found. | Markdown/CSV evidence logs, redaction workflows. | Claim-source audit before delivery. | Unsupported claims, doxxing, ambiguous allegations. | [GOV-03: Berkeley Protocol on Digital Open Source Investigations](https://www.ohchr.org/en/publications/policy-and-methodological-publications/berkeley-protocol-digital-open-source-investigations); [PRO-04: The Verification Handbook](https://verificationhandbook.com/) | Upgrade report templates. |
