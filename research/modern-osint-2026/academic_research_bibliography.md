## Practical implications for OSINT skill updates

1. Academic OSINT survey work supports a taxonomy of collection, processing, analysis, and dissemination rather than a flat list of tools ([ACAD-01: The Not Yet Exploited Goldmine of OSINT: Opportunities, Open Challenges and Future Trends](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9139412)).
2. Information-disorder research supports precise labels and source/claim separation ([ACAD-03: Information Disorder: Toward an interdisciplinary framework for research and policymaking](https://rm.coe.int/information-disorder-report-november-2017/1680764666), [ACAD-04: Defining “Fake News”: A typology of scholarly definitions](https://doi.org/10.1080/21670811.2017.1360143)).
3. Misinformation research warns that virality and repetition can bias analysts and audiences ([ACAD-05: The spread of true and false news online](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aap9559), [ACAD-06: Misinformation and Its Correction: Continued Influence and Successful Debiasing](https://doi.org/10.1177/1529100612451018), [ACAD-08: The science of fake news](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aao2998)).
4. Crowd/source-quality research can help triage reliability but does not replace primary-source verification ([ACAD-07: Fighting misinformation on social media using crowdsourced judgments of news source quality](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1806781116)).
5. Cyber and social-media OSINT literature is fragmented; the skill should cite specific method sources where possible and maintain a “future literature expansion” section rather than overclaim from broad search routes ([ACAD-09: OSINT as Part of Cyber Defense: systematic and survey literature](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=OSINT+cybersecurity+survey+open+source+intelligence), [ACAD-10: Social Media Intelligence: privacy and methodological concerns](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=social+media+intelligence+OSINT+privacy+methodology), [ACAD-11: Digital Open Source Investigations: methodological and evidentiary research](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=digital+open+source+investigations+evidence+chain+of+custody)).
